About this site!

Newthy is an artificial intelligence powered article summarizer website constructed with intent of providing reliable information within a bite size. The reliability factor is determined by a system devised to collect the most commonly repeated topics, then generate a summary based on them. Through the "Greatest Common Factor" style of filtering and summarizing, a user can read up on information that is most likely true to the amount of repetitions for said information.
